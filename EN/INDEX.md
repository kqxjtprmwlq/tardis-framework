## üìù How to contribute

The project is open to:
- **Formal verification** of derivations
- **Completing open derivations** (5.2, 5.3 in formal.md)
- **Implementations** in specific domains
- **Structural critiques** via explicit negation of axioms
- **Alternative functional architectures**

Completeness is not sought, but collaborative development under epistemic rigor.
